Calculating ROI for Predictive Maintenance in Discrete Manufacturing

By NeuroForge AI • Aug 1, 2025 • 6 min read

When planning a predictive maintenance (PdM) program, the most important question is simple: will it pay off?

This article provides a lightweight ROI model that balances downtime costs, false positive impacts, and crew constraints.

Assumptions

  • Average downtime cost per hour
  • Historical failure rate and mean time to repair
  • Model precision and recall

Quick formula

ROI = (Avoided Downtime Cost - PdM Operating Cost - False Positive Cost) / Investment

We recommend sensitivity analysis across precision/recall and crew availability.
