A Practical Guide to Website AI Assistants

By NeuroForge AI • Aug 1, 2025 • 5 min read

Website AI assistants can reduce support costs, increase conversions, and provide 24/7 product knowledge. Success depends on the right data and guardrails.

What to index

Docs, product catalogs, FAQs, policies, and CRM context. Use a vector store with metadata filters.

Privacy & auth

Implement role-based access, PII redaction, and audit logs. Never send secrets to third-party providers.

Measuring impact

Track containment rate, lead quality, and time-to-resolution. Run A/B tests against baselines.
